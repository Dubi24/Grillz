Who We Are

Born on the Streets

We cook with fire & passion

GRILLZ started as a single food stall in 2018 with one mission — serve food so good it stops people in their tracks. No shortcuts, no frozen patties, no compromise.

Every ingredient is sourced fresh daily. Every burger is hand-pressed. Every wrap is rolled to order. We believe fast food shouldn't mean cheap food — it should mean food that's ready fast and tastes unforgettable.

From our spicy chicken wraps inspired by street markets, to our beast burger that's become a local legend — this is GRILLZ.
